Radamel Falcao

Radamel Falcao García Zárate (born 10 February 1986) is a Colombian professional footballer
who plays as a striker for La Liga club Rayo Vallecano and the Colombia national team.
Nicknamed "El Tigre" (The Tiger),[5][6][7][8] he is considered one of the best strikers of his
generation,[9][10] and as one of the greatest Colombian footballers of all time
